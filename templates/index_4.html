By express news serivce : price reduction by HUL from 21st may

Hindustan Unilever Limited (HUL) said it will reduce prices of personal hygiene products such as lifebuoy sanitizers, Lifebuoy liquid handwash and domex floor cleaners by 15 percent amid the coronavirus crisis. The country’s biggest consumer goods firm said it has committed a total of Rs 100 crore to help in the fight against the pandemic virus. “ In crisis like this, companies have a big role to play . we are working closely with the government and our partners to ensure that we overcome this global health crisis together,” said Sanjiv mehta., chairman and managing director, HUL.
